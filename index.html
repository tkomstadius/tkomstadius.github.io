NOW

My name is Therése and I currently live in Stockholm. I work as a developer at Com Hem. Com Hem is a company that delivers TV, telephony and broadband to swedish homes and businesses. I work with systems for our customer service. Our primary languages are Java and JavaScript and we try to use them in a functional way. We also apply a very agile and self organizing way of thinking.

HOW

I just entered my second year as an employee in the IT-industry. Before that I was a cashier at a company that manages cash. I was employed there for seven years. It was a job that required teamwork, vigilance, mental arithmetic and a lot of problem solving.

In 2010 I started studying Cognitive Science at Linköping's University, LiU. After one year I felt that this was not right for me so in 2012 I began studying Media Technology, also at LiU. After three years of math and a lot of programming, I took my Bachelor's degree i Technology.

LET IT SNOW

This is a project me and four classmates did at university. We did snow simulation for the visualization dome at Norrköpings visualization center. This was done using C++ and OpenGL. We also created a simple control panel using C#.

BOUNCE

Another project from university. Me and three classmates created a model for stiffness in objects using a weight-spring approach. Then we tried simulate this with a visualization. We tried doing this with different degrees of stiffness. We used C++ and OpenGL.

SLEEP DEPRIVED

In 2015 I attended Hack For Sweden. In my team we were three developers and a designer. We had 24 hours to come up with an idea using goverment bodies open data. The idea that we had was to create a platform to bring together people looking for employment with buisnesses seeking employees. No personal information other than the qualifications for the job would be available at initial contact to avoid bias and discrimination.
